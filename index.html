Hi, thanks for taking the time to know me. My name is Steven. Just a recent college graduate living in Seattle looking for job opportunities.
‍
I stumbled upon human-centered design a few years ago and fell in love. I like the idea of listening to people and getting to know them, then using my skillset to help. To me, design is a way to form connections and create positive change. I know that I’m not the best, nor will I ever be, but I’m always willing to listen and learn.

In my spare time I like to go out on adventures with my camera, capturing the beauty of life and the world around us. My favorite photographer is Diane Arbus because she was able to humanize the people she photographed, capturing their emotion in its sincerest form.

Good design comes from collaboration, so if you want to chat about design hit me up on LinkedIn. I’m always eager to meet new people!
